 ##📊 Business Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the **Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Internship (Task 1)**.

The objective of this project was to analyze business sales data and develop an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that provides meaningful insights into sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product categories, and regional performance. The dashboard helps businesses make data-driven decisions through clear visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Build an interactive business sales dashboard.
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Compare product categories and customer segments.
- Identify profitable regions.
- Track monthly sales trends.
- Generate actionable business insights.

## 🚀 Dashboard Features

- Interactive KPI Cards for Total Sales, Profit, Orders, and Customers.
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis.
- Sales by Product Category.
- Profit by Region.
- Sales Distribution by Customer Segment.
- Top 10 Products by Sales.
- Interactive slicers for Year, Region, Category, and Segment.
- Dynamic filtering for easy business analysis.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

- 📈 **November** recorded the highest monthly sales.
- 📅 **September, November, and December** were the strongest revenue months.
- 💻 **Technology** generated the highest sales among all product categories.
- 🌍 **West Region** produced the highest overall profit.
- 👥 **Consumer Segment** contributed the largest share of total sales.
- 🪑 **Furniture** and **Office Supplies** also performed well but generated lower sales than Technology.
